About AL-JAMEATUL QADRIYA

Our History, Mission, and Leadership

Current Principal

Hazrat Allama Maulana Muhammad Khalilullah Qadri

Guardian of Ahl-e-Sunnat, Renowned Scholar and Thinker

Under his dedicated leadership, AL-JAMEATUL QADRIYA has grown from humble beginnings to become a beacon of Islamic education, combining traditional Islamic studies with modern primary education.

Our History

"Knowledge is Light" - The fundamental objective of the madrasa is the propagation of Islamic teachings. This is why the Creator commanded the Prophet to read first. Establishing schools and madrasas in this region to eradicate educational backwardness and introduce people to the religion is extremely necessary.

With this objective in mind, on December 31, 1990, esteemed personalities in the land of Jaltolia initiated a madrasa. Foremost among these distinguished individuals were Hazrat Hafiz Abdul Shakoor and Hazrat Hafiz Muhammad Jasar Hussain.

Initially established as a madrasa named Jamia Qadriya Rizvia Naeemia, it began its journey with basic education on makeshift arrangements. For higher education, Hazrat Muhammad Jasar Hussain selected a dedicated individual on July 11, 1991, entrusting him with the leadership of the madrasa.

This devoted person laid the foundation of the madrasa on April 11, 1992, naming the institution after the great saint Syedna Sheikh Abdul Qadir Jilani (RA) and renaming it as Al-Jamia Al-Qadriya.
